The Art of Terminal Design
======================

Posted: 2024-01-15

A deep dive into creating retro-style interfaces that capture the essence
of classic computing while maintaining modern usability standards.

The terminal interface has been a cornerstone of computing since its inception.
Even in today's GUI-dominated world, there's something uniquely appealing
about the simplicity and directness of command-line interfaces.

Key aspects of good terminal design:
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Consistent color scheme
- Readable typography
- Responsive feedback
- Intuitive commands

Modern terminals can combine the best of both worlds: the efficiency of
command-line interfaces with the interactivity of modern web applications.
